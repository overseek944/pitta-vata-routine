# 12 · Shatavari — Complete Guide (choosing, buying, taking)

**Shatavari (*Asparagus racemosus*)** is the classic Ayurvedic women's tonic — **sweet, cooling, and unctuous (oily)**, which is *exactly* why it suits a Pitta–Vata woman: it **cools Pitta**, **nourishes & grounds Vata**, and supports **hormonal / reproductive balance and regular, less-painful cycles.**

> Introduce it **alone**, start **low**, and — since it's for reproductive support — ideally clear it with a **doctor/vaidya**, especially if on medication or with a hormone-sensitive condition (PCOS etc.). See [`DISCLAIMER.md`](./DISCLAIMER.md).

---

## 1. Forms — which to choose

| Form | Verdict |
|---|---|
| **Churna (pure root powder)** ⭐ | Best & most flexible; mix in warm water/almond milk + ghee |
| **Capsules / tablets** (e.g. Himalaya) | Convenient; just swallow — good if she dislikes the powder taste |
| **Shatavari Kalp / "granules"** | ⚠️ **Avoid for daily use** — mostly **sugar + cardamom**, not pure root |
| **Shatavari ghrita** (medicated ghee) | Nourishing/grounding for Vata; optional, richer |

**Insist on:** true **Asparagus racemosus, root**, 100% single-herb, **no added sugar**, no "stem/extract" blend, no fillers.

- Sealed; clear mfg/expiry + batch number

## 4. How to take it

### If using CHURNA (powder)
- **½–1 tsp (≈3–5 g)** once or twice daily.
- Mix into **warm water** or **warm almond milk + ½ tsp ghee + pinch of cardamom**.
- Warm + ghee boosts the Vata-nourishing, grounding effect (dairy-free).

### If using HIMALAYA TABLETS (or any tablet/capsule)
- You **swallow** it — can't mix into milk.
- **Dose:** follow the label (often 1 tablet twice daily). **Start with 1 after breakfast** for 3–4 days, then add a second.
- Take **after food**, with **warm water** (or warm almond milk).
- Keep the **ghee separately** in her meal/bedtime drink — the tablet and ghee both work; they needn't be in the same spoon.

### ⭐ Simplest two-time schedule (recommended)
> **Morning:** 1 Shatavari (tablet or ½–1 tsp churna) **after breakfast, with warm water.**
> **Night:** 1 Shatavari **with the bedtime drink — warm almond milk + ½ tsp ghee + pinch nutmeg** (this doubles as her sleep drink, so almond milk is only needed once a day).

**Do you *need* almond milk?** No — **warm water is fine.** Almond milk is an optional, nourishing "carrier" (nice for Vata), not a requirement. Don't drink a big glass of milk/almond milk *with* a salty/sour main meal (poor Ayurvedic combo) — keep it to morning or night.

---

## 5. Timeline & cautions
- Works **gradually** — give it **4–8 weeks** of consistent daily use.
- **Don't over-dose** ("more = faster" doesn't apply).
- Slightly **Kapha-increasing** — not ideal with heavy congestion/mucus (not a concern for a Pitta–Vata picture).
- Introduce **one herb at a time**; stop if any adverse effect; check with a professional for reproductive/medication concerns.
