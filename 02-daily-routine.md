# 02 · The Master Daily Routine (Dinacharya)

This is your wake-to-sleep template, **made dairy-free**. Timings are guides — *consistency of timing matters more than the exact clock.* Adjust to your life, then keep them stable.

> Re-read the north star: **Warm-not-hot · Moist & oily · Regular & calm · Sweet/grounding · Ghee.**

## 🌅 MORNING

### 1. Wake gently (~6:00–6:30 am)
- Wake near sunrise. Avoid a jarring alarm (jars Vata).
- Stay in bed 1 min: 5 slow breaths, a calm intention. **No phone.**

### 2. Cleanse mouth & senses
1. Splash **cool water** on face and eyes.
2. **Brush** teeth.
3. **Tongue scraping** — 5–7 gentle front-to-back-cleared strokes. Removes *ama* (toxic residue), wakes digestion.
4. **Oil pulling (optional):** swish 1 tsp coconut oil 5–10 min, spit in trash, rinse.

### 3. Warm water (the constipation trigger)
- **1–2 glasses (250–500 ml) warm — not hot — water**, sipped steadily, empty stomach.
- Best version: **fennel water** or **soaked-raisin water**. Avoid strong lemon (too sour for Pitta).

### 4. Toilet / bowel training (~10–20 min later)
- Sit at the **same time daily**, even without urge — this *trains* the colon.
- **Footstool** (knees above hips) for the natural squat angle. **Never strain;** breathe slowly.

### 5. Aloe vera juice
- **15–20 ml** inner-leaf (aloin-reduced) aloe in **room-temp/lukewarm water**, empty stomach (~10 min after warm water).
- Cooling (Pitta) + mildly laxative (Vata). Do **not** combine with senna.

### 6. Abhyanga — warm-oil self-massage ⭐ (your single best practice)
1. Warm the oil (bottle in hot water). **Oil:** coconut or Brahmi in heat; a little sesame in cold.
2. Massage **5–10 min:** long strokes on limbs, circular on joints, **clockwise on the belly (helps bowels)**, plus **scalp, ears, soles of feet** (deeply calming for Vata).
3. **Optional Nasya:** 2–3 drops cow ghee or Anu taila in each nostril, sniff up.
4. Let it absorb **10–15 min** (do light stretches meanwhile).

### 7. Warm shower
- **Comfortably warm, never very hot.** Leave a little oil on scalp/skin.

### 8. Yoga sequence (~25–35 min)
Full details in [`04-yoga-sequence.md`](./04-yoga-sequence.md). Go **slow, smooth-breathed, grounding — never rushed or overheating.**

### 9. Pranayama (~8–10 min)
Full details in [`05-pranayama-and-meditation.md`](./05-pranayama-and-meditation.md):
- Nadi Shodhana (6–9 rounds) → Bhramari (5–7) → Sheetali (3–5, modest).

### 10. Meditation (10–15 min)
- Breath-awareness or calming guided/mantra. This is where you tell Pitta it can stop pushing and Vata it is safe to settle.

### 11. Breakfast (~8:15–9:00, warm & dairy-free)
Pick one:
- **Cooked oats/porridge in water or almond milk** + ghee + soaked raisins/dates + cardamom.
- **Stewed apple/pear** + cinnamon + a few soaked, peeled almonds.
- **Soaked-and-cooked figs/prunes/raisins** + warm fennel tea.
- Savory: **moong dal cheela** or mild **poha**.
- ❌ Skip: cold cereal, dry toast alone, iced smoothies, sour curd, fried items.

## ☀️ MIDDAY

### Lunch = biggest meal (~12:30–1:30 pm)
Your digestive fire peaks midday. See [`03-food-guide.md`](./03-food-guide.md) for plate-building.
- **Grain** (rice / soft chapati) + **mung dal/lentils** + **cooked veg with ghee** + **1–2 tsp ghee**.
- Optional dairy-free digestive sip: warm **CCF tea** (cumin-coriander-fennel).
- Spices: cumin, coriander, fennel, turmeric, mild ginger — **no chili**.

**After lunch:** sit in **Vajrasana 3–5 min**, then a **gentle 5–10 min walk** ("shatpavali"). **No daytime sleep** (a 10–15 min seated rest only if truly drained).

### Hydration through the day
- **Warm/room-temp water**, **CCF tea**, **coconut water**. Target **2.5–3 L**, more in heat.
- ❌ No iced drinks, minimal/no coffee, no alcohol/soda.

### Optional afternoon snack (~4–5 pm)
- Soaked dates/figs, a sweet ripe fruit, soaked nuts, or fennel tea + a date. Prevents the Pitta "hunger-anger" dip before dinner.

## 🌆 EVENING

### Light movement
- Calm **15–20 min walk** in greenery, or a few restorative poses (Child's pose, Legs-up-the-wall).
- ❌ No intense late workouts (over-stimulates both doshas before bed).

### Dinner (~6:30–7:30 pm — light & early)
- **Best: Kichari** (rice + mung dal + ghee + mild spices) — the perfect Pitta–Vata, easy-to-digest, bowel-friendly meal.
- Alternatives: vegetable soup + soft rice, dal-rice, cooked veg + chapati.
- Finish **2.5–3 hrs before bed.** Short stroll after.
- ❌ Avoid heavy, fried, spicy, sour, raw, or very late dinners.

---

## 🌙 NIGHT & SLEEP

### Wind-down (~8:30–9:30 pm)
1. **Dim lights; screens off ~45–60 min before bed.**
2. **Bedtime drink (dairy-free):** **warm water + 1 tsp ghee + pinch of nutmeg** (nutmeg = the natural sleep aid). *Optional:* use warm **almond/oat milk** instead of water if you want a milky feel.
3. **Triphala:** ½–1 tsp powder (or 2 tablets) in warm water. Gentle overnight bowel regulation.
4. **Foot & scalp massage** with a little warm oil — 2–3 min. Profoundly calming for Vata.
5. **3–5 rounds of Bhramari** or slow belly breathing in bed.

### Sleep (~10:00–10:30 pm)
- Lights out by 10:30. Sleep is non-negotiable — its lack spikes **both** doshas. Cool, dark, quiet room.

---

## Quick Do / Don't

**Do:** regular timings · warm-cooked-moist food · ghee daily · sweet/grounding tastes · oil massage · early light dinner · sleep by 10:30 · calm gentle movement · plenty of warm water.

**Don't:** skip meals · spicy/sour/fried/fermented · ice-cold or raw-cold food · coffee/alcohol/smoking · late nights · intense or hot exercise · over-schedule/multitask · strain on the toilet · push to exhaustion.
