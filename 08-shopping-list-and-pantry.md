# 08 · Shopping List & Pantry

Stock these once and the whole plan becomes effortless. Everything here is **dairy-free** except **ghee** (kept intentionally — see [`03-food-guide.md`](./03-food-guide.md)).

---

## 🛒 Grains & legumes
- [ ] Basmati rice
- [ ] Split yellow mung dal (moong dal) — *your staple protein*
- [ ] Rolled oats
- [ ] Atta (whole wheat flour) for soft chapati
- [ ] Barley (optional)

## 🥬 Vegetables (buy fresh, cook soft)
- [ ] Lauki (bottle gourd)
- [ ] Carrot
- [ ] Beetroot
- [ ] Pumpkin
- [ ] Sweet potato
- [ ] Zucchini
- [ ] Green beans
- [ ] Spinach
- [ ] Asparagus (optional)
- *Avoid stocking:* raw-salad items as staples, lots of tomato/eggplant, chilies, raw onion/garlic for raw use.

## 🍎 Fruit & dried fruit
- [ ] Pears
- [ ] Apples (for stewing)
- [ ] Bananas (ripe)
- [ ] Pomegranate / melon (seasonal)
- [ ] Dates
- [ ] Figs (dried, to soak)
- [ ] Prunes (to soak)
- [ ] Raisins (to soak)
- [ ] Almonds (to soak & peel)
- [ ] Walnuts (small amounts)

## 🫙 Fats & "milk"
- [ ] **Ghee** ⭐ (your main fat — keep good quality)
- [ ] Coconut oil (cooking + oil pulling + massage in heat)
- [ ] Olive oil (optional)
- [ ] Almond milk and/or oat milk (unsweetened) — dairy-free creaminess

## 🌿 Spices (your Pitta–Vata-friendly set)
- [ ] Cumin (jeera) — seeds + powder
- [ ] Coriander (dhania) — seeds + powder
- [ ] Fennel (saunf)
- [ ] Cardamom (elaichi)
- [ ] Turmeric (haldi)
- [ ] Saffron (optional)
- [ ] Nutmeg (jaiphal) — *for sleep at bedtime*
- [ ] Cinnamon
- [ ] Fresh ginger (use small amounts, cooked)
- [ ] Rock salt (sendha namak) — use modestly
- *Avoid stocking as staples:* chili powder, mustard, excess black pepper, lots of asafoetida.

## 🍵 Drinks & sweet
- [ ] Loose fennel / cumin / coriander (for CCF & fennel teas)
- [ ] Coconut water (fresh if possible)
- [ ] Rock sugar / mishri (small amounts) or rely on dates/raisins
- [ ] **Gulkand** (rose-petal jam)

## 🌱 Herbs & remedies
- [ ] **Triphala** (powder or tablets) — bedtime
- [ ] **Aloe vera juice** (inner-leaf, aloin-reduced, food grade) — morning
- [ ] **Isabgol** (psyllium husk)
- [ ] **Shatavari** (powder/capsule)
- [ ] **Brahmi** (powder/capsule, optional)
- [ ] **Castor oil** (small bottle — occasional escalation only)
- [ ] Massage oil: **Brahmi oil** and/or plain sesame (cold weather) / coconut (hot weather)
- [ ] Anu taila or cow ghee for optional **Nasya**

## 🧰 Tools
- [ ] **Tongue scraper** (copper or steel)
- [ ] **Toilet footstool** (knees-above-hips for natural squat)
- [ ] Yoga mat
- [ ] Small kettle/pot for warm water & teas
- [ ] A thermos to keep warm water handy through the day

---

## Pantry logic (why these, in one line each)
- **Mung dal + basmati rice + ghee** = the kichari base, the most Pitta–Vata-balancing meal.
- **Soaked dried fruit** = gentle fiber + moisture for constipation (no harsh laxatives).
- **Cumin/coriander/fennel** = cooling digestives that calm Pitta and de-gas Vata.
- **Ghee + Triphala + aloe + isabgol** = your lubricate-and-regulate constipation kit.
- **Nutmeg + Brahmi + foot-massage oil** = the sleep & calm kit for the nervous system.
