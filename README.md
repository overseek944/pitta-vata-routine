# 🌿 Pitta–Vata Balancing & Constipation-Relief Routine

A complete, detailed Ayurvedic lifestyle plan built for a **Pitta + Vata constitution with a constipation tendency** — and made **dairy-free / milk-free** (ghee is kept, since clarified ghee is essentially lactose- and casein-free).

> **North star (the one rule behind everything here):**
> **Warm — but not hot · Moist & oily — not dry · Regular & calm — not erratic or intense · Sweet & grounding — over spicy/sour · Ghee is your medicine.**

---

## Why this plan exists

This person has an **excess Pitta + Vata** imbalance, which shows up as two seemingly opposite sets of symptoms at once:

- **Pitta (fire + water):** heat, acidity, irritability, frustration, burnout, sharp hunger.
- **Vata (air + space):** dryness, **constipation**, anxiety/worry, irregularity, depletion, fatigue.

Because the two doshas pull in opposite directions (Vata wants *warm/oily/heavy*, Pitta wants *cool/light*), the whole plan is engineered around the **overlap zone** that calms both at the same time. See [`01-understanding-pitta-vata.md`](./01-understanding-pitta-vata.md).

## Key personalization notes

- ✅ **Milk-free:** no cow's milk anywhere. Ghee is retained (clarified — virtually no lactose/casein). Where a "milk drink" is useful, **warm water + ghee** or **almond/oat milk** is used instead.
- ✅ **Hot-climate aware:** tuned for a hot region (e.g. Gujarat) — extra hydration, cooling tilt within the warm-not-hot rule.
- ✅ **Constipation-first:** lubricating (ghee, soaked fruit, Triphala, isabgol, aloe) rather than harsh heating stimulant laxatives like senna.
