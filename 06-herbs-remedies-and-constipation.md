# 06 · Herbs, Remedies & Constipation Protocol

All **dairy-free** (herbs taken in warm water or almond milk, never cow's milk). For a Pitta–Vata gut, the strategy is **lubricate and regulate — not stimulate and purge.**

> ⚠️ See [`DISCLAIMER.md`](./DISCLAIMER.md). Start one herb at a time, low dose, and watch how you respond. Check with a doctor/vaidya if you take medications or have a health condition.

---

## Core herbs & schedule

| Herb | When | Dose | Why / what it helps | Why it suits Pitta–Vata |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ghee** ⭐ | Lunch, dinner, bedtime | 1–2 tsp each (≤2–3 tsp/day total) | Lubricates the colon, eases stool, nourishes tissues | Cooling enough for Pitta, oily/grounding for Vata — the perfect bridge food |
| **Triphala** | Bedtime | ½–1 tsp powder or 2 tablets in warm water | Gentle bowel regulation, mild detox, tones the gut | Tridoshic; contains cooling **Amla** so it won't overheat Pitta; gentle enough for Vata |
| **Aloe vera juice** | Morning, empty stomach | 15–20 ml in warm water | Cooling, mild laxative, soothes gut lining | Cools Pitta + gently moves Vata constipation |
| **Isabgol (psyllium husk)** | Bedtime (or as needed) | 1–2 tsp in warm water/almond milk | Bulk-forming, softens & moves stool | Neutral & moistening — safe for both; **must take with enough water** |
| **Shatavari** | Morning or night | as labeled, in warm water/almond milk | Nourishing, cooling, calming, rejuvenating | Sweet + cooling + grounding = made for Pitta–Vata depletion |
| **Brahmi** | Day/evening | as labeled | Calms the mind, supports sleep & focus | Cooling for Pitta, settling for Vata anxiety |
| **Gulkand (rose-petal jam)** | Anytime | ½–1 tsp | Cooling, mild laxative, soothes acidity | A tasty Pitta cooler that's gentle on Vata |
| **Soaked dried fruit** (figs, prunes, raisins, dates) | Morning | 3–5 pieces, soaked overnight | Natural fiber + moisture → softer stool | Sweet + moist = calms both; no harsh stimulation |

### ⚠️ Use only occasionally (escalation, not daily)
| Remedy | When | Dose | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Castor oil (Eranda)** | Stubborn constipation, at night | 1–2 tsp in warm water/almond milk | Classic Vata-constipation purgative; effective but don't make it a daily habit |

### ❌ Avoid for routine use
- **Senna / "Laxher" / harsh stimulant laxatives** — heating (aggravates Pitta) and drying/depleting on rebound (aggravates Vata). Fine as a rare one-off if truly stuck; **not** a daily tool for you.
- Strong, hot, drying herbs and heavy "detox" cleanses.

## Daily constipation toolkit (gentle, layered)
This is your everyday baseline — already built into the routine:
1. **Warm water** on waking (rehydrates the colon, triggers the gastrocolic reflex).
2. **Soaked fruit** at breakfast (fiber + moisture).
3. **Ghee** at meals + bedtime (lubrication).
4. **Movement** — yoga (Cat-Cow, twists, Pavanamuktasana) + post-meal walks.
5. **Same-time toilet sit** with a footstool, no straining (trains the colon).
6. **Triphala** at night (regulation).
7. **2.5–3 L warm/room-temp water** through the day.

---

## Constipation escalation protocol
Use only if the daily toolkit isn't enough on a given day.

- **Day 1 (no movement):** add extra **2 tsp ghee in warm water** at night + **1–2 tsp isabgol** with plenty of warm water.
- **Day 2:** add **soaked figs/prunes** morning and evening; ensure full hydration; do **Pavanamuktasana + twists**.
- **Day 3 (still stuck):** **castor oil 1–2 tsp** in warm water/almond milk at night (occasional only).
- **Persistent (>1–2 weeks), or any pain / blood / weight loss / vomiting → see a doctor.** Do not keep self-treating.

## Cooling teas (dairy-free, anytime)
- **CCF tea:** ¼–½ tsp each of **C**umin + **C**oriander + **F**ennel, simmered 5 min, strained. Aids digestion, gentle, balancing.
- **Fennel tea:** ½ tsp fennel simmered — cooling, anti-gas, Pitta-friendly.
- **Coriander tea:** cooling, good for acidity.
