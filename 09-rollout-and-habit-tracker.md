# 09 · Rollout Plan & Habit Tracker

For your Pitta–Vata constitution, **how** you adopt this matters as much as the plan itself. Trying to do everything perfectly on Day 1 is *Pitta* behavior — and it leads to burnout (then you quit). Layer it in instead.

> **Consistency > completeness.** A few habits done daily beat the whole plan done once.

---

## 🪜 4-Week phased rollout

### Week 1 — Foundation (timing + hydration)
- [ ] Wake & sleep at consistent times (sleep by ~10:30)
- [ ] Warm water on waking
- [ ] Same-time toilet sit (footstool, no strain)
- [ ] Eat 3 meals at regular times — **never skip**
- [ ] 1 tsp ghee daily
- *Why first:* establishes the regularity Vata needs and stops the meal-skipping that flares Pitta — the biggest wins for least effort.

### Week 2 — Cleanse + move
- [ ] Tongue scraping each morning
- [ ] The yoga sequence (even a 15-min short version)
- [ ] Triphala at bedtime
- [ ] Post-lunch short walk
- *Why now:* gentle digestion + movement build on the timing base and get the bowels moving.

### Week 3 — Calm + self-care
- [ ] Abhyanga (oil massage) — even 5 min
- [ ] Pranayama (Nadi Shodhana + Bhramari)
- [ ] 10-min meditation
- [ ] Aloe vera juice in the morning
- *Why now:* with structure in place, add the nervous-system care that treats the emotional core (Pitta burnout + Vata anxiety).

### Week 4 — Refine + lock in
- [ ] Full dairy-free food plan (favor/reduce lists)
- [ ] Dinner light & by ~7:30
- [ ] Bedtime ritual (ghee-water + nutmeg + foot massage)
- [ ] Add Shatavari/Brahmi if desired
- *Why last:* fine-tune once the basics are automatic, so nothing feels overwhelming.

By the end of Week 4, most of this is simply *how you live* — not a checklist you fight.

## 📓 Weekly check-in (5 questions, every Sunday)
1. How many days did I have a comfortable bowel movement?
2. Pitta signs this week (acidity, heat, irritability, burnout) — better or worse?
3. Vata signs this week (constipation, gas, dryness, anxiety, sleep) — better or worse?
4. Which 3 habits helped most? (Protect these.)
5. What will I keep simple next week?

---

## 🚦 Quick self-diagnosis (which way am I tipping?)

| If you notice… | You're tipping toward… | Do more of… |
|---|---|---|
| Acidity, heat, anger, impatience, skin flare | **Pitta** | Cooling foods, Sheetali, Gulkand, forward bends, less spice, avoid midday sun |
| Constipation, gas, dryness, anxiety, insomnia, restlessness | **Vata** | Warm oily food, ghee, abhyanga, grounding poses, early bed, more routine |
| Both at once | **Pitta–Vata** | The core plan as written: warm-not-hot, moist, sweet, calm, regular |

---

## A final reminder
The goal isn't a flawless tracker. It's a calmer gut, calmer mind, and regular bowels — reached **gently**. If a day goes sideways, just return to the backbone the next morning. **Returning, not perfecting, is the skill.**
